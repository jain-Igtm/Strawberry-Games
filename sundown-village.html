Sundown Village

This version drops the fake 2D shooter and concentrates on the actual place: scale, sightlines, porches, buildings, lights, grass, and the oval end of the Western town.

Mercantile storefronts run down the left side of the grassy flat. Cabins and porch fronts mirror them on the right. The barn, church, and red shed close the oval at the far end beneath the trees.

Desktop: WASD, mouse, Shift. Mobile: left stick to walk, drag the right side to look.
